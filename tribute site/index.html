itachi uchiha

The boy who sacrificed everything

who was itachi uchicha:

  • background: he was the first son of the uchiha's clan leader.graduated from the academy in just 4 months.awakened sharingan at the age of 10.
  • personality: he was very calm and insightful person. he was humble and kind towards other.
  • Abilities: with his mangekyou sharingan, he could use Amaterasu and tsukuyomi. itachi could also use susanoo wielded with totsuka blade and yata mirror.
  • intelligence: itachi was highly intelligent individual.he thought very quickly and remain calm in a fight.he was always calculative and methodical in his approaches.

"People live their lives bound by what they accept as correct and true… that is how they define reality. But what does it mean to be correct or true? Merely vague concepts… their reality may all be an illusion."

--itachi uchicha
